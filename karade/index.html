Pillar I — Radical Devotion (Auset)

Weeks 1–5 • Alignment • Discipline • Inner Authority

0%
1

Orientation: What Are You Devoted To?

Devotion vs obedience • Identify false devotions • Begin daily stillness.

Inner Work

  • Devotional inventory: time, energy, habits.
  • 10 minutes daily stillness (same time each day).
  • Identify “false devotion” patterns (approval, fear, ego, distraction).

Outer Expression

  • Write a one-paragraph “why I’m here” statement.
  • Choose one daily act that symbolizes devotion (simple + consistent).

Legacy Question

Would my ancestors recognize what I’m devoted to?

2

Discipline as Sacred Technology

Consistency over motivation • Discipline = freedom • Build your 7-day cycle.

Inner Work

  • Create a 7-day discipline cycle (sleep, food, movement, study).
  • Remove one habit that weakens devotion.

Outer Expression

  • Track your cycle daily (1–2 lines).
  • Declare one boundary that protects your practice.

Legacy Question

What would change in my lineage if I became consistent?

3

Inner Authority & Self-Sovereignty

End dependency • Restore inner law • Become accountable to self + ancestors.

Inner Work

  • Write a personal code of conduct (5–10 lines).
  • Identify where authority is misplaced (people, fear, comfort).

Outer Expression

  • Practice one act of self-leadership daily.
  • Stop one repeating excuse phrase.

Legacy Question

Where have I surrendered my crown?

4

Devotion in Action

Integrity • Private/public alignment • Devotion becomes behavior.

Inner Work

  • Behavioral audit: where do I contradict myself?
  • Correct one recurring contradiction.

Outer Expression

  • Replace one weak habit with one strong habit.
  • Do one “quiet discipline” action (no announcement).

Legacy Question

If my descendants copied me, would I be proud?

5

Auset Integration

Stabilize devotion • Confirm discipline • Prepare speech + wisdom pillars.

Inner Work

  • Review weeks 1–4 notes.
  • Write: “My Devotional Standard” (1 page max).

Outer Expression

  • Share a brief recap (oral or written) with your cohort.
  • Set 3 non-negotiables for the next 11 weeks.

Legacy Question

What does “Radical Devotion” look like in my daily life?

Pillar II — Words of Power (Sekert)

Weeks 6–10 • Speech • Silence • Creative Command

0%
6

Speech as Creation

Words are technology • Prayer/command • Replace careless speech.

Inner Work

  • Begin “speech awareness”: catch filler + self-cursing phrases.
  • Rewrite affirmations with precision.

Outer Expression

  • Speech fast: remove one category (gossip, complaining, doom-talk).
  • Speak one daily declaration aligned with devotion.

Legacy Question

Do my words build the lineage—or weaken it?

7

Silence, Restraint, Listening

Silence is power • Listening to ancestors • Restraint as mastery.

Inner Work

  • One weekly silence block (30–60 minutes minimum).
  • Journal what arises in silence.

Outer Expression

  • Practice “pause before reply” (3 breaths).
  • Remove one reactive response pattern.

Legacy Question

What truth do I avoid hearing?

8

Writing as Transmission

Write to preserve • Document the path • Leave records, not rumors.

Inner Work

  • Begin your personal SGI text (journal/treatise).
  • Write 1 page this week (max 500 words if you prefer).

Outer Expression

  • Create a “teach-back” paragraph (what I learned + how I apply it).
  • Organize notes into headings (future legacy archive).

Legacy Question

What must be written so it can’t be erased?

9

Teaching, Naming, Correction

Teach responsibly • Correct without cruelty • Truth without ego.

Inner Work

  • Identify where your speech harms (tone, sarcasm, dominance).
  • Define “correction” vs “humiliation.”

Outer Expression

  • Deliver a short teaching (2–5 minutes or 1 page).
  • Practice one corrective conversation with composure.

Legacy Question

Does my voice heal—or control?

10

Sekert Integration

Voice stabilized • Restraint refined • Speech aligned with devotion.

Inner Work

  • Review weeks 6–9 notes.
  • Write: “My Speech Standard” (10 lines).

Outer Expression

  • Record one teaching (audio/video or written).
  • Create a personal “Words of Power” script (short).

Legacy Question

What does my lineage sound like through my mouth?

Pillar III — Infinite Wisdom (Tehuti)

Weeks 11–15 • Study • Integration • Applied Intelligence

0%
11

Wisdom vs Information

Principles over quotes • Study as sacred labor • End intellectual laziness.

Inner Work

  • Choose 1 text or system to study consistently.
  • Extract 3 principles (not just “facts”).

Outer Expression

  • Write a 1-page “principle summary.”
  • Apply one principle within 24 hours.

Legacy Question

What do I actually know well enough to live?

12

Ancestral Intelligence

Beyond names • Systems + methods • Reverence → embodiment.

Inner Work

  • Research one ancestral system (Ifa/Khemet/diaspora).
  • Identify what must be continued and what must be corrected.

Outer Expression

  • Create a 5-bullet “Ancestral Intelligence” brief.
  • Teach one insight to someone (or write it publicly).

Legacy Question

What am I doing that proves gratitude?

13

Comparative Thinking

Patterns across systems • Avoid dogma • Disciplined synthesis.

Inner Work

  • Compare two systems: where do they agree in principle?
  • Identify your personal “bias lenses.”

Outer Expression

  • Write a short comparative analysis (1 page or 3 minutes spoken).
  • List 3 universal principles you can apply immediately.

Legacy Question

Can I unify without diluting?

14

Wisdom Applied to Life

Health • Finances • Relationships • Leadership • Results matter.

Inner Work

  • Select 1 real-life problem to address with a principle.
  • Identify the “one move” that changes momentum.

Outer Expression

  • Apply the principle for 7 days.
  • Document outcomes: what improved, what resisted, what changed.

Legacy Question

Does my wisdom show up in my lifestyle?

15

Tehuti Integration

Applied intelligence • Demonstrate results • Prepare legacy week.

Inner Work

  • Review weeks 11–14 and summarize 5 principles you now live.
  • Write: “My Wisdom Standard” (10 lines).

Outer Expression

  • Deliver a final teach-back: devotion → speech → wisdom alignment.
  • Create a simple plan for continued study (next 90 days).

Legacy Question

What will I be known for—knowledge or transformation?

Week 16 — Integration & Legacy

Extending Royal Legacy • Standing on the Shoulders of Enlightened Ancestors

0%
16

Crown + Continuity: Become an Ancestor Worth Standing On

Synthesize pillars • Publish legacy • Commit to ongoing discipline.

Extending Royal Legacy

  • Royalty = conduct + responsibility.
  • Define what you’re building that outlives you.
  • Create a structure: routine, archive, teaching, service.

Standing on Enlightened Ancestors

  • Move from gratitude to continuation.
  • Identify 3 ancestors (blood or spiritual) and their carried principles.
  • Declare how you will refine and extend what they began.

Final Deliverables

  • Legacy Statement (1 page).
  • SGI Path Declaration (10 lines).
  • 90-Day Continuation Plan (simple checklist).

Course Outcome: Disciplined (Auset) • Conscious Speech (Sekert) • Applied Wisdom (Tehuti) • Legacy-Minded.

Course Doctrine (One Line)

We devote ourselves fully, speak with power, study without limits, extend royal legacy, and rise upon the foundations laid by enlightened ancestors.
